Dice game

Welcome to my dice game!

Here are the rules

  1. Roll a 6 on both dices to win the game!!
  2. Roll the same number on both dices (other than 6) to win three more free throws!
  3. If you don't get repeated numbers, do not despair. If the sum of both dices is even, you'll still have one more chance to win that wonderful bear ;)
  4. Any other combination is a lose.
